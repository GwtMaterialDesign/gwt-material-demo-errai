Panels

By setting shadow to 1 (setShadow(1)) to any material panel, material row, material column, material card, you can easily apply a shadow to that element

<m:MaterialColumn backgroundColor="WHITE" grid="s12 m12 l2" shadow="1" waves="DEFAULT"/> <m:MaterialColumn backgroundColor="WHITE" grid="s12 m12 l2" shadow="2" waves="RED"/> <m:MaterialColumn backgroundColor="WHITE" grid="s12 m12 l2" shadow="3" waves="TEAL"/> <m:MaterialColumn backgroundColor="WHITE" grid="s12 m12 l2" shadow="4" waves="YELLOW"/> <m:MaterialColumn backgroundColor="WHITE" grid="s12 m12 l2" shadow="5" waves="PURPLE"/>
Any Material Elements

You can directly add shadows on any material widgets by adding: addStyleNames='z-depth-1' to the ui binder widget.

<m:MaterialImage resource="{res.image}" addStyleNames="z-depth-1"/>
